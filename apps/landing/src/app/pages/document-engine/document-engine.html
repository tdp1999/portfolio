Why build one at all.

Document generation is a solved problem right up until the documents matter. In regulated work the editor stops being a text box and becomes part of the product, and that is where a rented one runs out of room.

Two packages, on purpose.

The split is the design. A document model that imports nothing can be read on a server, validated in a test, and migrated by a script. Everything framework-shaped is quarantined in the binding.
