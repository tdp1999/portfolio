Lightbox — full-screen viewer + zoom/carousel

Opt-in per figure: add lightbox to any landing-figure and it becomes clickable into a full-screen viewer. Figures sharing a lightboxGroup navigate together — swipe / drag / ←→ keys / filmstrip. The overlay loads the largest available candidate (srcset always-best) and supports double-tap / pinch / ⌘-wheel zoom, pan-when-zoomed, and a FLIP open/close from the thumbnail. Clickable figures signal themselves on hover: cursor: zoom-in plus a subtle image zoom (motion-gated).

Grouped — click any to open the carousel

All four share lightboxGroup="case-study", so opening one lets you swipe/arrow through the set. Try double-tap (touch) / double-click (mouse) / ⌘+scroll to zoom, then drag to pan.

Standalone — no group

A lone figure opens on its own (no prev/next, no filmstrip). Download is opt-in (lightboxShowDownload) — enabled here, hidden by default elsewhere.

Inputs (directive)

  • lightbox — enable (bare attribute). [lightbox]="false" disables.
  • lightboxGroup — group key; figures sharing it navigate together (default "default").
  • lightboxFullSrc · lightboxSrcset — explicit full-res source for the overlay.
  • lightboxShowDownload — show the download action (hidden by default). lightboxDownload — its URL (falls back to the resolved full source).
  • lightboxSrc · lightboxAlt · lightboxCaption — for bare (non-figure) hosts.
