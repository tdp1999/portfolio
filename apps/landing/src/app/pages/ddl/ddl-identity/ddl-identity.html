Guidelines · do / don't

The locked rules for using the marks. Recolour the accent per product — that's config. Everything else here is fixed so the Brand stays itself across every surface.

Clearspace

Keep a margin of at least the Monogram's cap-height clear on every side. Nothing — type, edges, other logos — crowds the mark.

Minimum size

16px · floor
32px+ · clean

16px is the favicon floor — legible but tight. Prefer 32px and up. Never set the Monogram below 16px.

✓ do

Recolour the accent per product — the Dot is the one themable atom.

✓ do

Use knockout on a solid accent fill; mono on busy / low-contrast surfaces.

✓ do

Let the ink inherit currentColor — the mark adapts to light & dark.

✗ don't

Don't stretch, squash, or distort the proportions.

✗ don't

Don't rotate or tilt the mark.

✗ don't

Don't add shadows, glows, gradients, or outlines.

✗ don't

Don't recolour the letters — only the accent Dot carries colour.

✗ don't

Don't put the full mark on a low-contrast background — switch to mono / knockout.
