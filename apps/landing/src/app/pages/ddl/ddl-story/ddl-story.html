A · Manuscript
Pure literary essay. Prose IS the design. Most appropriate when the section is meant as a "moment of pause" and asymmetry doesn't need to be preserved.
B · Journal
Engineering-notebook register. Active marginalia and chip sync make the reading feel guided, technical. Best for a portfolio that wants to feel like a workshop, not a magazine.
C · Spotlight
Most cinematic. C₁ requires a real B&W/duotone portrait — highest emotional payoff. C₂ keeps the same atmospherics with an abstract mark — no asset dependency.
D · Artifact
Most personal. Reads as a diary entry, dated and signed. The timeline grounds the prose in concrete shipped artifacts. Most intimate of the four.
