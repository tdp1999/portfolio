Feed — item variants.

Three shape families for any feed/index page (/projects, /blog, future). Side-by-side on the same 4-project sample. Pick one as default; view-toggle in the toolbar lets users switch. Marks: 🟢 recommend, 💡 candidate, ⚠ fallback only.
