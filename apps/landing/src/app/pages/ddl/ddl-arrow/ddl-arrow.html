Three takes on a tired pattern

Hover each row. B (Lift-off) is the chosen pattern, now live on landing-link & landing-button. A and C are kept here as design references. Reduced-motion users get the static state.

A · Connector line

B · Lift-off (with ghost) — shipped

C · Letter-press hand-off

Text shifts left 1px, arrow shifts right 3px. No new elements, no color change. Maximum restraint — feels tactile, almost imperceptible until you notice.

landing-icon-arrow is composed inside button / link, not placed alone. The two glyphs (right, up-right) carry semantic weight: same-domain vs leaving the site. Don't mix.

Indigo budget. A page gets one indigo moment. The arrow's lift-off animation, the active link state, and the solid button background all draw from the same budget — pick one.
