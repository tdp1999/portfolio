Chips are right for finite enum sets of 5–15 options. For other shapes the bar should mix component types — chips alone are not enough. Examples:

  • Year · chips fine for 5–10 years. For 15+ → group as decade chips ("2020s", "2010s") or a range slider.
  • Status (enum, 5 values) · chips perfect.
  • Stack (~20 distinct) · chips OK when category-grouped (top-N per category, as this demo does). Becomes noisy at 50+ → autocomplete combobox.
  • Free-text search · <input>, not chips. Could complement chips (search + filter pills side-by-side).
  • Date range · date picker (e.g., a future <landing-date-range>).
  • Boolean toggle ("Featured only", "Has source code") · <input type="checkbox"> styled as a switch, not a chip.

For V1 portfolio scale, everything is chips. Mixed component support gets added when /blog (tag/category facets) or future search lands.
