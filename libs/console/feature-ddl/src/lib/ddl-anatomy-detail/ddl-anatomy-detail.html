Realestatedoc Console

Published
ID: p1 Created 3 weeks ago Updated 2 days ago by Phuong Tran

Admin console for a bootstrapped Singapore real-estate startup.

The team was running a property pipeline out of a spreadsheet and a group chat. I wanted to see whether a small, opinionated console could replace both without a migration weekend.

Sole engineer. Data model, API, console, and the deploy pipeline behind it.
Challenge

Agents edited listings on-site where signal dropped, and lost work.

Approach

Queued mutations locally and reconciled on reconnect, last-writer-wins per field.

Outcome

Lost-edit reports stopped entirely.
Challenge

Who changed an asking price, and when, was unanswerable after the fact.

Outcome

Cut the pricing-dispute thread to a single screen.

2 of 3 fields written in English.
